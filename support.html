Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Pure Dispatch?

Sign up at pure-dispatch.vercel.app, complete your carrier registration with MC/DOT numbers, and subscribe to a plan. Pure will guide you through the setup process.

What subscription plans are available?

We offer two plans: Monthly at $39.99/month and Annual at $399/year (save 17%). Both include unlimited load searches, AI-powered dispatch, and all core features.

How does the AI calling feature work?

Pure uses advanced AI (powered by ElevenLabs and Twilio) to call brokers on your behalf, negotiate rates, and confirm load details. All calls are recorded for your records and quality assurance.

Which load boards does Pure Dispatch integrate with?

We're launching with DAT and 123Loadboard integration in Q1-Q2 2026. Additional load boards will be added based on user demand.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption (TLS/SSL), secure password hashing, and industry-standard security practices. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Access continues until the end of your billing period. No refunds for partial months.

Does Pure Dispatch work on mobile?

Yes! Pure Dispatch works on any device with a web browser. For the best experience, use Chrome or Safari on iOS/Android.

How do I verify my carrier credentials?

During registration, enter your MC and DOT numbers. Pure automatically verifies them with FMCSA. You'll see a green checkmark when verified.

What if I have a problem with a load or broker?

Contact us immediately at contact@puredispatch.co.site. We'll review call recordings and help resolve disputes. However, Pure Dispatch is not responsible for broker actions.

Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds on a case-by-case basis within 7 days of initial subscription. Contact contact@puredispatch.co.site to request a refund.
