Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Pure Dispatch ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered virtual dispatcher service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Carrier Information: Company name, MC number, DOT number, insurance details
  • Driver Information: Driver's license, medical card, CDL information
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely via Stripe)
  • Documents: COI, NOA, W-9, POD, rate confirmations

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Location Data: Real-time GPS coordinates when you enable tracking
  • Usage Data: Features used, loads viewed, messages sent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Call Recordings: AI-generated calls to brokers (for quality assurance)
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • FMCSA Data: Carrier safety ratings, authority status, insurance verification
  • Load Board Data: Load information from DAT, 123Loadboard, and other partners
  • Payment Processors: Transaction data from Stripe

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Pure Dispatch service
  • Match you with relevant loads based on your preferences
  • Communicate with brokers on your behalf
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Verify your carrier credentials via FMCSA
  • Track your location for navigation and route optimization
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send service updates and important notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI algorithms
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Your Consent

  • Brokers and Shippers: When you book a load, we share your contact info and location
  • Load Boards: We query load boards on your behalf using your credentials

3.2 Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Twilio: Voice calling services
  • ElevenLabs: AI voice synthesis
  • Vercel/AWS: Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Google Maps: Navigation and routing services

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.

3.4 Business Transfers

If Pure Dispatch is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Correction

You can view and update your account information in the app settings at any time.

5.2 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format by emailing contact@puredispatch.co.site.

5.3 Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. We will delete your personal data within 30 days, except data we're legally required to retain.

5.4 Opt-Out Rights

  • Location Tracking: Disable GPS in app settings
  • Marketing Emails: Unsubscribe via link in emails
  • AI Calling: Disable auto-calling in app settings

5.5 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

5.6 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have additional rights including:

  • Right to access, rectify, and erase personal data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion + 30 days
  • Call Recordings: 12 months
  • Location History: 24 months
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Support Tickets: 3 years

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Preferences and settings storage
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Security and fraud prevention

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Pure Dispatch is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we've collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers in the United States. By using Pure Dispatch, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via:

  • Email to your registered address
  • In-app notification
  • Prominent notice on our website
